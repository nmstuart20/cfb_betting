Expected Value (EV)

Expected value represents the average return per dollar wagered. A positive EV bet indicates the sportsbook odds are better than the true probability, providing a long-term edge. Calculated by comparing predictive model probabilities against implied odds.

Arbitrage Betting

Arbitrage opportunities occur when betting all outcomes across different sportsbooks guarantees profit regardless of result. Happens when combined implied probabilities are less than 100%. Stakes are calculated to lock in guaranteed returns.
